About Us

Discover the story behind LAVA JAVA Café.

Founded in the heart of Georgia in 2020, LAVA JAVA Café brings a fusion of traditional and modern coffee experiences to coffee aficionados and casual café goers alike. Our mission is to create a cozy environment where everyone can enjoy high-quality coffee, freshly baked goods, and an assortment of light bites. With a commitment to sustainability, we source our coffee beans from local and international small-scale farmers, ensuring every cup supports not just our community but also those around the globe.
